## Quick Start Guide

1. **Update Personal Information:**
   - Open `components/sections/Hero.tsx` and replace "Your Name" with your actual name
   - Update your professional title and description
   - Replace placeholder social media links with your actual profiles

2. **Add Your Experience:**
   - Open `components/sections/Experience.tsx`
   - Replace the `experiences` array with your actual work history from your resume
   - Include company names, job titles, dates, and achievements

3. **Update Skills:**
   - Open `components/sections/Skills.tsx`
   - Modify the `skillCategories` array with your actual technical skills

4. **Add Your Projects:**
   - Open `components/sections/Projects.tsx`
   - Update the `projects` array with your real projects
   - Add project descriptions, tech stacks, and links

5. **Update About Section:**
   - Open `components/sections/About.tsx`
   - Replace placeholder text with your actual education and professional story

6. **Update Contact Info:**
   - Open `components/sections/Contact.tsx`
   - Replace email, location, and social links with your actual information

7. **Update Footer:**
   - Open `components/Footer.tsx`
   - Replace placeholder links and information

8. **Update SEO Metadata:**
   - Open `app/layout.tsx`
   - Update the metadata object with your information

## Resume Integration

Since you mentioned attaching your resume, here's how to integrate it:

1. Extract key information from your resume:
   - Name, title, contact info
   - Education history
   - Work experience (companies, roles, dates, responsibilities)
   - Skills and technologies
   - Projects you've worked on
   - Freelancing experience (if any)

2. Map resume sections to components:
   - Personal Info → Hero.tsx
   - Education → About.tsx
   - Work History → Experience.tsx
   - Skills → Skills.tsx
   - Projects → Projects.tsx
   - Services → Freelancing.tsx
   - Contact → Contact.tsx
